Introduction

Injuries can happen anytime. Knowing the first things to do can save lives. Always prioritize safety and seek professional help if needed. This site provides accurate, general advice based on standard first aid principles.

Common Injuries and Quick Tips

  • Cuts and Scrapes: Clean, apply antibiotic ointment, and bandage.
  • Burns: Cool with running water for 10 minutes; cover loosely.
  • Sprains: Rest, ice, compress, elevate (RICE method).
  • Broken Bones: Immobilize the area and seek immediate medical help.
